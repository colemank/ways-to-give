Facebook for FUTURES
Facebook offers an easy way to launch a fundraiser on your page and encourage your friends and family to support Futures Without Violence. You can create a Facebook fundraiser to:
  • Ask for donations to FUTURES in lieu of birthday gifts.
  • Raise money in memory of a loved one.
  • Highlight an important event, such as Sexual Assault Awareness Month, Domestic Violence Awareness Month, Giving Tuesday, etc.
Give Through Your Work

Workplace Giving

Many employers have workplace giving programs. You can choose Futures Without Violence to receivea specific tax-deductible donation through automatic payroll deductions. Make this a one-time gift, or support us through monthly, bi-monthly, or weekly installments.

Federal employees: If you are a federal worker, you can participate in the Combined Federal Campaign. Choose to donate to Futures Without Violence by checking off 11662 on your CFC enrollment card.

Employer-Matching Gifts

Did you know that many employers and companies sponsor matching gift programs for their employees? That means your tax-deductible gift to Futures Without Violence could be doubled or even tripled! If your company participates in matching gift programs, request the appropriate form, complete it, and mail it in with your donation. We will take care of the rest.
